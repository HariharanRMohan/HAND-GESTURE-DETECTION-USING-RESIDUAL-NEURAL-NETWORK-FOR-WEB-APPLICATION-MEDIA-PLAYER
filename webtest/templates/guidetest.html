How To Use The Gesture System

How To Use The System

1. First of all, open the website and wait for the frame appear.

2. Then, if you didn't allow camera to use, please allow it.

3. Next, refer to the guide page on what gestures that can be performed.

4. Later, go back to the main page and click play button to play YouTube.

5. Finally, control the YouTube player using your hand gestures.

HAVE FUN!!!

List Of Gesture To Control The Media Player

1.Drumming Fingers Function:Play And Pause!!!

2.Sliding Two Fingers Up Function:Volume Up!!!

3.Sliding Two Fingers Down Function:Volume Down!!!

4.Stop Sign Function:Volume Mute!!!

5.Swiping Hand To The Left: Prev!!!

6.Swiping Hand To The Right: Next!!!
